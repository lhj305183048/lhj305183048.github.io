KEEP LEARNING

a learning assistance and companion system

Let your learning process no longer be lonely and helpless. The personalized smart chatbot and online study room help you find your learning partners and better plan your learning progress.

AI Assistant

Design your own chatbot to assist and accompany you in your study process. You can personalize it and set it to speak like your favorite characters.

Study Room

Join or create a study room, find online study partners, and discuss subjects of common interest and problems you encounter

Four Assistant Functions

A variety of learning functions provided by AI conversation: scheduling, taking notes, memorizing vocabulary, and managing progress; More features in development.
